What is Bluebird Coin?

Bluebird Coin is a meme-based cryptocurrency inspired by Matt Furie’s "Boys Club", featuring characters like Pepe, Andy, Wolf, and Brett.

You can purchase Bluebird Coins on decentralized exchanges like Uniswap. Simply connect your wallet and trade Ethereum (in BASE chain) for Bluebird Coins.
